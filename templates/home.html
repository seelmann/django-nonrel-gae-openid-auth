Django-nonrel Google App Engine OpenID Auth Example

This example demonstrates how to setup OpenID authentication for Django-nonrel on Goole App Engine. The source code of this little example is available at http://github.com/seelmann/django-nonrel-gae-openid-auth

Click the Login link below, it opens the login form where you can enter your OpenID URL. Once authenticated your name and a Logout link will be displayed.

Please note: When you login your OpenID URL, Name, Nickname, and Email address will be stored in the the App Engine data store!
